The Story

We Met

Beijing, 2010

As the U.S. economy sank to new lows in the depths of the Great Recession, both Bion and Diana found themselves fleeing to teach English at the Beijing Royal School.

Diana was asked to greet Bion the night he arrived and make sure he got settled in, but she took Bus 3 going the wrong direction and didn’t make it ‘til late. Bion, never alerted that Diana was meant to greet him, was surprised when someone knocked on the door as he was about to shower, but relieved to discover she spoke English, and had a working phone he could use in the morning.

When we think about how lucky it is that our paths crossed, we’d really like to give a shout-out to Wall Street and real estate speculators everywhere.

We Dated (Again)

Missoula, 2012

Bion had just returned to the Northwest after a year in Kunming. Diana had just moved to Montana to complete her MFA in creative writing.

Bion g-chatted Diana up: “A friend who is far away is sometimes much nearer than one who is at hand. Is not the mountain far more awe-inspiring and more clearly visible to one passing through the valley than to those who inhabit the mountain?”

He drove through mountain passes and brought her 25 pounds of chocolate. She made him a leek and mushroom quiche.

We’re Getting Married

Seattle, June 24, 2017

And we hope you’ll join us to celebrate.

The Details

The ceremony will be held on June 24, 2017
at Islandwood in Washington State

Islandwood is a 255 acre non-profit environmental learning center on Bainbridge Island. It has modern on-site lodging and easy access via public transit from SeaTac airport.

While the ceremony is held on June 24, the use of the entire facility is reserved from the evening of June 23 through the morning of June 25. You are invited to spend both nights on-site and enjoy the use of the space. Over the weekend we'll offer meals and activities.

All proceeds from the wedding and your rooms will go to support Islandwood's non-profit outdoor education programs for public school children.
