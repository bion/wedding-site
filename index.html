Details

The ceremony will be held on June 24, 2017
at Islandwood in Washington State

Islandwood is a 255 acre non-profit environmental learning center on Bainbridge Island. It has modern on-site lodging for our guests and easy access via public transit from SeaTac airport.

While the ceremony is held on June 24, the use of the entire facility is reserved from the evening of June 23 through the morning June 25. Guests are invited to spend both nights on-site and enjoy the use of the space. Over the weekend we'll offer meals and activities.

All proceeds from the wedding will go to support Islandwood's non-profit outdoor education programs for public school children.
